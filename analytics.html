💡 Key Insights for Executive Leadership

🎯

Event-Driven = 9.9% of incidents but causes 26% of road closures. Disproportionate impact vs volume — targeted resource allocation pays off big.

⏰

Peak congestion window: 20:00–22:00. Evening events (IPL, festivals, processions) are the primary drivers. Pre-deploy resources by 18:00.

📍

North Zone 1 & East Zone 1 are the highest event-density zones. ORR East 2 corridor has 102 event-driven incidents — needs permanent rapid-response capability.

🏗️

Construction is 60% of event-driven congestion (480 events) and 65% is planned. Huge opportunity for advance coordination with BBMP/utility companies.

Pre-Event Intelligence Briefing: For every planned event (57% of event-driven incidents), generate an automated impact forecast + resource plan 24h before.

Construction Coordination Protocol: Require BBMP/utility companies to register road work 48h in advance. Auto-generate traffic management plans.
